Gardening Tips

Use Compost to Enrich Soil

Composting not only reduces waste but also provides rich nutrients for your garden.

Choose Native Plants

Native plants require less water and are more resistant to local pests and diseases.

Water Early or Late

Watering in the morning or evening reduces evaporation and helps plants absorb moisture more effectively.
